What are websites?

Websites are pages of information that people can view on the World Wide Web.

How are websites made?

Websites are most commonly created using a Markup Language such as HTML. This Markup Language describes what content will be displayed to a screen on a web browser.

How do some websites look so nice?

Style Sheet Langauges such as CSS are connnected to HTMl files. They describe how to style the content on the webpage and make it look less basic.
